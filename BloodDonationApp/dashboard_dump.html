Donation Guidelines

  • Ensure you are well-hydrated and have had a healthy meal before donating.
  • You must weigh at least 110 lbs (50 kg) and be in good general health.
  • A minimum interval of 56 days is required between whole blood donations.
  • Bring a valid government-issued photo ID to the donation clinic.
